Project Overview

Project Scope and Features

  • Forward Geocoding: Converts addresses into geographical coordinates (latitude and longitude).
  • Reverse Geocoding: Converts coordinates into human-readable addresses.
  • Weather Forecasting: Provides current weather conditions including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation chance.
  • Mapping: Displays locations on an interactive map, allowing users to visualize geographical data. The map also includes an option to automatically detect the user's current location.

Tips for Users

  • Ensure accuracy in entering addresses or coordinates for precise results.
  • Utilize the map functionalities to explore and visualize geographical data effectively.
  • Optimize weather reports by providing accurate location coordinates.

Conclusion

Rudra-Geo aims to simplify access to geolocation and weather information, catering to users' needs for accurate and timely data retrieval.
